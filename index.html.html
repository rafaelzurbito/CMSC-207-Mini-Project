About Me

Rafael Zurbito (a.k.a. Raffy) is a professional who has more than 10 years of experience in property management, facilities management and project management. He has managed end-to-end operations of high-rise residential condominiums. His work involve: legal and general administration; accounting and finance; engineering and maintenance; housekeeping and sanitation; security and safety with the overall objective of enhancing property value and improving the lives of the community he manages.

Raffy is also interested in technology and digital tools, utilizing these for process improvement by identifying and removing bottle necks, documentation, and communication. He values integrity and a firm believer of growth mindset. Strives to integrate both technical and organizational skills to support effective decision-making.

Outside of his career, Raffy is a family man with two children. He strives to maintain a work-life balance ensuring to still have time for family and other activities.
